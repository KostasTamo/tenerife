About me

Hello, welcome to my page, which I created to share some of the photos I took during the time I spent abroad in Tenerife. I'm not a professional photographer, and I take these photos only for myself and to make some memories that I can share online with other people. The "Photos" section shows that I really enjoy snapping pictures of the outdoors and scenic settings, especially those filled with gorgeous flowers and foliage.
